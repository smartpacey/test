Payroll courses

  • Cources
  • Payroll courses

The objective of Payroll Course is to provide students with a complete knowledge of Payroll Management using Marg Pay Software.

This course has been designed to assist not only people involved solely in payroll management but also for small business owners and business people looking to get the edge over their competitors. It provides you how to manage time tracking, payroll processing and paying employees, benefits, payroll taxes and tax remittances. It also discusses how to deal with payroll accounting and how to set up a system of procedures, controls and recordkeeping.
